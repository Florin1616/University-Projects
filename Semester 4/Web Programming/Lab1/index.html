Our History

Specialized Bicycle Components, Inc. was founded in 1974 by Mike Sinyard in California. It began as an importer of Italian bicycle parts. In 1976, Specialized introduced the Allez, one of the first production bikes with an aluminum frame. In 1981, they released the Stumpjumper, the first mass-produced mountain bike, which helped popularize the sport. Since then, Specialized has been at the forefront of innovation, introducing technologies like the Body Geometry fitting system and FSR suspension. Today, Specialized is a leading global brand known for its high-performance bicycles and commitment to innovation.
